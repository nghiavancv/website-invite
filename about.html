Wedding Planning and Co.

As a company started in 2000, Jim and Pam wanted an affordable and easy wedding but they could not find any that seemed to fit the criteria. Using this as motivation, the couple founded Wedding Planning and Co. to help other couples plan their weddings with no stress!
